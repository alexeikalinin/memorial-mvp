FAQ

Questions we get asked.

Is this disrespectful to the deceased? +
We designed Memorial around preservation, not simulation. The avatar only speaks from what your family explicitly shared — it's a vessel for real memories, not a replacement for a person. Many families find it a deeply healing way to stay connected to someone they loved.
What if the AI says something out of character? +
The avatar is grounded only in memories you upload — it won't invent facts or fabricate events. You can review all responses, and if anything feels off, simply add or edit memories to guide the avatar. You always have full control.
Can multiple family members contribute memories? +
Yes. Share an invite link with any family member — they can add photos, videos, and text memories without creating an account. Designed specifically for gathering contributions at funerals, reunions, or from across the world.
How does voice cloning work? Do I need a studio recording? +
Any audio works — a WhatsApp voice message, a home video, a birthday recording. Upload at least one minute and we'll create a voice model. No studio required. The more audio you provide, the more accurate the clone.
What happens to my data if I cancel? +
Export all your memories, photos, videos, and chat transcripts at any time — no lock-in. We will never delete a memorial without 90 days notice and multiple export opportunities. The Lifetime plan includes permanent hosting guarantees.
Do I need any technical skills to set this up? +
None at all. Upload a photo, type a memory — the AI does the rest. Most families have a working, talking memorial within 10 minutes. No coding, no configuration, no technical knowledge required.
