# Optional live B-roll (2–3 shots)

Use when cutting a **director’s cut** or ads. Not required for the landing page build.

## Shot 1 — Hands + print

- **Framing:** Top-down or 45°; hands only; no identifiable faces unless model-released.
- **Action:** Turn a page in a small photo album, or lift a framed print from a dark surface.
- **Lighting:** One key at 45°, **3200–4000K** softbox or window + curtain; white card fill; **optional** cool rim at 5% from behind.
- **Background:** `#080810`–`#13131f` matte board or fabric.

## Shot 2 — Phone dark → light

- **Framing:** Phone face-down on table; **slow** lift to reveal a warm home screen (blur UI if not product).
- **Lighting:** Same as Shot 1; add a **small practical** (warm LED) reflected on the screen edge.

## Shot 3 — Candle / warm bokeh (abstract)

- **Framing:** Out-of-focus flame or LED “candle”; foreground dark.
- **Safety:** Real flame only with fire-safe setup; LED fake candle is fine.

## Grade

- Desaturate slightly (match landing hero portrait ~20% grayscale feel).
- Keep blacks in **legal** range; avoid crushed noise.
